# Invite Privacy Policy

*Last updated: May 2026*

## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Invite, a product of Chiera Technologies.

Invite respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Invite mobile application.

By using Invite, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide event management and guest verification services.

### Identity Information

* Full name
* Email address

### Event Information

* Event titles
* Event descriptions
* Event venue details
* Event schedules

### Guest Information

* Guest names
* Guest email addresses
* Verification photos (if provided)

### Technical Information

* Device information
* Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) tokens
* App usage analytics
* Crash reports

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

* Create and manage events
* Generate secure invitation QR codes
* Verify guest attendance
* Send event-related notifications
* Improve app performance and security
* Provide analytics to event hosts
* Prevent fraud and unauthorized event access

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information.

Guest photos and event data are securely stored using Firebase services with restricted access controls.

Despite our efforts, no electronic transmission or storage method is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share limited data with trusted third-party services used to operate the platform, including:

* Firebase services by Google
* Notification delivery services

These services are only used to support Invite functionality.
